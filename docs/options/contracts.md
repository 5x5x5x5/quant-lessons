# The options contract

> **Status:** Draft pending.

**Scope:** Define call and put options: strike $K$, expiry $T$, premium. Draw the payoff diagrams at expiry for long call / long put / short call / short put. American vs European, cash vs physical settlement, and why equity index options are typically European cash-settled.

**You'll be able to reason about:**

- Why every option has a non-negative price — the right to not exercise has positive value.
- Why deeper-OTM options are cheaper in absolute terms but offer higher leverage to moves that matter.
- The distinction between **intrinsic value** (what you'd get exercising now) and **time value** (everything else).
